Bubu's Profile

Name : Bubu
Gender : Female
Date of Birth : 31st October, 2015
Place of Birth : Hong Kong
Weight : 3.8kg
Favourite : Canned Food

Bubu's Story

Bubu was a little brown tabby cat with striking black stripes that ran down her back. She was born in October of 2015 as a stray, spending her early months wandering the streets in search of food and shelter. But Bubu's luck changed one day when she met a young couple who were out for a walk.

The couple noticed Bubu right away, her big green eyes and playful demeanor capturing their hearts. They approached her slowly, offering some food and water, and before they knew it, Bubu had curled up in their laps for a nap.

From that day on, the couple couldn't resist Bubu's charm, and they decided to adopt her as their own. Bubu quickly adapted to her new home, playing with toys, snuggling up on the couch, and exploring every nook and cranny of her new surroundings. With her new family, Bubu finally had the love and care she deserved.
